What is this website for?

This website is initially made for lurkers who like clicking on random links, but more specifically it's targeted towards employers as it's essentially the portal to my CV/Resume and Portfolio, as well as my socials. Even if you're not an employer and just a stranger on the internet, you are welcome to take a look at my hard work!

How did you make it?

I did not use any sort of web designer, this is purely made in HTML, CSS and JS. I had planned my website a month before actually getting around to making it, and using web designer tools became really impractical and more complicated for what I wanted to achieve; It would also be ironic for a programmer like myself to use no-code web designers. This took around 30-ish total hours to complete, over the course of a month; It was really fun to make and I highly encourage others to make their own website!
