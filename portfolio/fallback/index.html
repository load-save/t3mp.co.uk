Games

I have a lot of projects that I'm currently working on, so here are a few that you can read about!

"Lily Fricking Sucks" is an atmospheric puzzle adventure game where players navigate a world filled with secrets as they unravel the lore surrounding Lily and her mysterious world. This is a passion project between me and my brother.

Monkeyaim is a web-based FPS aim trainer with multiple modes that focus on various techniques, such as flicking, speed, accuracy, and tracking. As a gamer myself, I find that all the web-based aim trainers aren't to my liking, so I decided to try and make one of my own.

"A Game About a Ghost" is a rogue-like fighting game where you unlock moves as you progress in the game, with an interesting gameplay mechanic of typing words to perform them. For example, to dash, you have to type "dash," and the faster you type it, the further you dash.

In Debate vs. Club, you challenge your friends to debate on fun topics and let an AI judge which side makes a stronger case. Rack up points for your team with convincing arguments and may the best debaters win! A very fun online party game, and a very new project of mine.
